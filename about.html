About ILoveEdit

ILoveEdit is a free, modern, and privacy-first online PDF toolkit designed for everyone. Whether you need to merge files, split pages, sign contracts, or convert between formats — we’ve got you covered.

We believe PDF editing should be easy, fast, and fun. No installations. No waiting. No tracking. Everything runs in your browser — your files never leave your device.

Built with love for students, professionals, and businesses alike, ILoveEdit is constantly growing to support your document workflows.

We’re here to simplify your life, one PDF at a time.
